# Location Brainstorm — {{name}}

Dump everything you know about this location. The best settings aren't just
backdrops — they shape the characters who live there and the conflicts that
unfold within them.

Think about:
- What does it look, sound, smell, and feel like?
- What's the atmosphere or mood of this place?
- Who lives or works here? What's the social dynamic?
- What's the history of this place? How has it changed over time?
- What makes it significant to your story or characters?
- What conflicts or tensions exist here?
- How does this place affect the people who enter it?
- What secrets does it hold?

Write freely below.
