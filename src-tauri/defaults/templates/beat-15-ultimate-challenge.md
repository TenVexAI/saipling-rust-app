# Beat 15: Ultimate Challenge — Brainstorm

**What this beat does**: The Ultimate Challenge is the final major test before the
story plunges into its darkest territory. The protagonist faces the toughest
obstacle yet — one that demands everything they've learned. The outcome of this
challenge sets up the crisis of Act 3.

**Act**: 2 — The Unknown World

**Questions to consider**:
- What is the greatest challenge the protagonist has faced so far?
- How does this challenge require them to use everything they've gained?
- What is the cost of this confrontation?
- Does the protagonist succeed or fail — and what are the consequences?
- How does this beat transition the story toward the darkest moment?
