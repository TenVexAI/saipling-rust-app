# Beat 9: Initial Progress — Brainstorm

**What this beat does**: Initial Progress shows the protagonist gaining traction.
They're learning the rules of the new world, making headway toward their goal,
and building confidence. Things seem to be working — which makes the coming
setbacks all the more devastating.

**Act**: 2 — The Unknown World

**Questions to consider**:
- What early wins does the protagonist achieve?
- What new skills, knowledge, or resources do they gain?
- How does success begin to change their attitude or confidence?
- What warning signs are they overlooking?
- How does this progress advance the central conflict?
