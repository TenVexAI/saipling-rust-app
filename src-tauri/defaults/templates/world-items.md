# Item Brainstorm — {{name}}

Dump everything you know about this item. The most memorable objects in fiction
carry meaning beyond their physical form — they represent power, memory,
connection, or change.

Think about:
- What is it and what does it look like?
- What is its purpose or function?
- Who made it, and why?
- Who possesses it now, and how did they get it?
- What makes it special, valuable, or dangerous?
- What role does it play in your story?
- Does it have any history, legend, or emotional significance?
- How do different characters relate to it?

Write freely below.
